# References and Influences

This document lists key experiments, observations, and theoretical works that inform and constrain the Null Ring Chronology Bubble (NRCB) framework. It is not an exhaustive bibliography, but a grounding reference set.

---

- S. Chandrasekhar, *The Mathematical Theory of Black Holes* (Oxford University Press)

## Notes

This repository draws on standard results and interpretations from the above works.  
No claim of novelty is made regarding the individual components; novelty lies in the constrained synthesis of these ideas under modern observational limits.
